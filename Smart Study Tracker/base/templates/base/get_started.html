What is Smart Study?

Smart Study is a modern study tracking platform developed by Shivam Bhatt and his team (Shivansh Singla, Malika Arora, and Isha Jain) as part of their university mini project.

It provides an easy-to-use dashboard where students can organize their subjects, plan tasks, and monitor progress week by week with detailed insights.

Smart Study also tracks daily achievements and calculates overall subject completion percentages, helping students stay focused and achieve their academic goals.

In addition, motivational quotes are displayed to encourage a positive mindset and continuous improvement.
